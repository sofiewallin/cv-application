Introduction

I'm above all interested in UX/UI-design, front-end development, usability and accessibility. Do you share my passions and interests? Do you think your company could be a good fit for me? Projects

I've worked on a lot of projects as a professional web designer, graphic designer and front-end developer. However, my most recent work, that I am most proud of at the moment, are websites and applications that I've planned, designed and developed as a student at the university.

Skills

I always design with a purpose. My style is minimalistic, even conventional and I always start with the users point of view. In the end I want the websites I create to be usable and accessible with a clearly conveyed message. When I code I focus on the same simplicity and accessibility. In every aspect of the websites I create I want usability, whether it be in the browser or in my code base.

Experience

I have been a professional web designer for 9+ years but I've been making websites for much longer than that. Design have always been my main interest and I have a bachelor degree in Graphic Design and Communication. Learning to code has made it possible for me to implement my designs. As a web developer I've been primarily self taught, but as of 2020 I am studying at the university to further my skills in web development.
